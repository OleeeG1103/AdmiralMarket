ADMIRAL MARKETS GROUP AS
Leading online trading provider, who offers trading in Forex and CFDs, on stocks, indices, precious metals and energy.
What we do?

Admiral Markets Group is a leading online trading provider, who offer trading in Forex and CFDs, on stocks, indices, precious metals and energy. Admiral Markets pride themselves on their dedication to state-of-the-art software and quality of service, through which they aim to ensure that their clients receive the best, most transparent trading experience.

Through a combination of trading technology, competitive trading conditions, educational resources and excellent customer service, the Admiral Markets Group has expanded its reach to offer its services worldwide through its regulated trading companies.

Admiral Markets Group operating companies are authorised and regulated in their respective jurisdictions. In the UK by the Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), in Estonia by the Estonian Financial Supervision Authority, in Cyprus by the Cyprus Securities and Exchange Commission and in Australia by ASIC, the Australian Securities and Investments Commission.
